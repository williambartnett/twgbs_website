The store was founded in 1965 by Natalie Sheetz and Julie Robinson, who hired architect Robert J. McClenahan to design it.

Robert and Ann Foy bought the bookshop in 1999, after their six children were grown, and ran the shop until closing in 2016. Cheryl Bartnett, who worked in the shop part-time, reopened the shop in 2018 just down the road from the original location after her children were grown.
