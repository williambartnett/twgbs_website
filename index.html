The Webster Groves Bookshop has been a cornerstone of Old Webster since 1965, offering meticulously curated books, puzzles, and greeting cards for readers of all ages.

After closing in 2016, the community's outpouring of support brought us back in 2018 — just around the corner at 27 North Gore Avenue — with the same warm atmosphere and knowledgeable service that made the original shop feel like home.

We believe a great bookstore is foundational to a great community, and we're proud to keep that tradition alive in Webster Groves.
